How sales are tracked

Keep Finance for the raw ledger: manual spendings such as rent, utilities, payroll, and tax entries belong here, together with manual sales adjustments. Keep Reports for analytics, dashboards, and exports. Gross sales come from paid orders before discounts, while net sales come from final paid totals after discounts.
